Description

DARK - Smokey Bolder Taste coffee beans are a premium coffee blend that is carefully crafted to satisfy the taste buds of coffee lovers who crave a strong and bold flavor.

These beans are sourced from the finest coffee plantations, where the beans are grown under the perfect conditions to produce a rich, robust flavor.
