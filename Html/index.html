Our Story: Origin Coffee - Premium Coffee Roasters...

In the early 2000s, two friends, Alex and Ben, were working at a large corporate coffee company. They were both passionate about coffee but felt that the company was more focused on profit than on quality. They dreamed of starting their own coffee company that would prioritize the sourcing of high-quality beans and ethical business practices.

Sustainability & Success

Today, Mountain View Coffee Co. is a well-respected and successful company, known for its dedication to quality, ethics, and education. Alex and Ben are proud to be making a positive impact on the coffee industry and the world.

Improving lives and communities

Our beans are sourced directly from farmers, who are paid fair prices for their crops. This helps to improve their lives and communities, and ensures that the beans are of the highest quality. We also provide training and education programs to help farmers improve their techniques and yields.
